Meditation & Motivation

Our students go through many personal ups and downs, and with the weight, they carry while they study, it doesn't make life any easier.
One day a student can be an inspiration to those around him/her, the following day that same student is out of energy and has no motivation to do anything at all.
Sometimes we need a little time, time to gather our senses, time away from the busy world, the assignments, and those around us.
Sometimes all it takes is just the right amount of words placed in the correct order for us to regain that courage we had or need.
Find out how you, as a student, can meditate and be enlightened by a few motivational quotes/posters below.

What is it?



Meditation is primarily used to bring the mind into a blissful and relaxed state,
silencing the distractive thoughts,
and to elevate your awareness. The main concept is to focus attention on a “thing”.
The “thing” can be internal (breath, emotions, thoughts, etc.)
or external (a candle flame, an object, etc.).
The power of meditation can actually increase the working size of your brain.
People who consistently meditate experience new connections within their brains,
and an increased capacity to think.
Meditation is a powerful activity because it improves neural connections,
preventing the degeneration of your neurons and protects your hippocampus.
Meditation increases your mind power, and it is also a mind control method.


Act


There are many forms of meditation. One common act of meditation is " Walking Meditation ".
This is done by picking a quiet place for a stroll or walk. Then, as you begin walking, focus on the quality of each step.
Are your legs tense or relaxed?
Expand your awareness to notice your surroundings, what do you see?
Listen to whats going on around you. Left to its ownself, your mind can get negative real quick.
Look for the good in you. Get stressed by your own stress, or choose to have thoughts that boost your wellbeing.
Look for the positives during your walk. The blue sky, fresh air on your face, a child’s laugh.
Keeping an eye out for beauty is way more relaxing than staying caught in the hamster wheel of negativity.

Motivation is the inner power that pushes us towards taking action and towards achievement.
It is a strong desire to do and accomplish something that you find important. It is the drive, the energy, and the fire burning
inside you that keeps you going toward your goal.
